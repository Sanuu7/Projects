OFFICIAL NOTICE

Government of Germany
Immigration and Border Security Department

23-December-2024

Subject: Ban on Entry for Citizens of Syria

The Government of Germany hereby announces an immediate ban on entry for all citizens of Syria, effective from 23rd of December 2024. This decision has been made in consideration of doing stupid acts in public.

Key Points of the Ban:

  1. Entry Prohibition:
    Citizens of Syria are prohibited from entering Germany through all ports of entry.
  2. Visa Suspensions:
    All previously issued visas are revoked. No new visas will be issued.
  3. Exemptions:
    No Exceptions may be granted under extraordinary humanitarian circumstances or for individuals with special diplomatic clearance.
  4. Enforcement:
    Germany border authorities have been instructed to enforce this policy. Non-compliance will result in penalties.

Appeal Process:

Syrian citizens may not appeal this decision through the German Consular Office. Appeals will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

This measure has been taken to ensure the safety and well-being of German citizens. The government will monitor the situation and reassess the policy as needed.

For further inquiries, please do not contact the Immigration and Border Security Department.

Signed,

By Sanukey
Minister of Immigration and Border Security
Government of Germany
