  2. Visa Suspensions:

    Mairian border authorities have been instructed to enforce this policy. Non-compliance will result in penalties.

Appeal Process:

Antarkarka citizens may appeal this decision through the Mairian Consular Office. Appeals will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
